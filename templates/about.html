All About Me

Hi! My name is Olivia. Welcome to my personal hub. Feel free to read more to learn about me.

Background

My full name is Olivia Nwokoma. I am 22 years old and was born on January 31st, 2001 (#TeamAquarius). I was born in White Plains, NY and lived there until I was four years old, where I relocated to Germantown, Maryland. I still live there to this day. I have two sisters (11 and 18) and one brother who is 23. Here are the things I love (in this order):

  1. Coffees and lattes.
  2. Lofi and jazz music.
  3. Warm/tropical weather.
  4. Beaches. Not during nighttime though.
  5. Doing my makeup.
  6. Sunrises.

Most importantly, my absolute favorite color in the world is pink.

Hobbies and Interests

Rollerskating

The fact that I listed this first is hilarious. I don't own skates, have been to the rink maybe three times, and don't know a single trick. But I do plan on one day investing in my own skates and dedicating time to practicing skating weekly, because I really do enjoy it.

Painting

Another newer hobby. Hobbies are a very new thing for me because I've always been a ~work-school-sleep~ kind of person and let creative outlets fall to the wayside. I went to a school-sponsored Paint N' Sip and loved how relaxed and centered I felt when I was painting. I was given a bunch of extra supplies after the event and I've been using them ever since.

Reading

Self-explanatory. I love to read. Like most people, I don't do it as much as I did when I was a child because technology has messed with my attention span. But I do make it a point to curl up with a good novel every once in a while. Here are some books I'm reading at the moment:

  • The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho
  • Ugly Love by Colleen Hoover
  • The Power of Full Engagement by James E. Loehr and Tony Schwartz

Journaling

I love journaling because I've always been better getting my thoughts out on paper than verbalizing them. Writing is a good outlet for me and helps me clear my head.
